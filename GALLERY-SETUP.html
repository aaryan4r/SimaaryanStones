SIMAARYAN STONES — Gallery Setup Guide

📸 Persistent Gallery Setup

Follow these 3 steps (takes ~5 minutes) to enable photos that are saved permanently and visible to all visitors on your GitHub Pages website.

How it works: Photos are uploaded to ImgBB (free image host) and the list of photo links is saved to JSONBin (free JSON storage). When anyone opens your site, it loads the list from JSONBin and displays the photos from ImgBB — fully automated, no server needed.

1 Get a Free ImgBB API Key FREE

ImgBB stores your granite photos permanently for free.

  • Go to imgbb.com and create a free account
  • After login, go to api.imgbb.com
  • Click "Get API Key"
  • Copy the key — it looks like: a1b2c3d4e5f6789012345678abcdef01
💡 Tip: Photos uploaded via this API are permanent and publicly accessible — perfect for a business gallery.

2 Create a Free JSONBin FREE

JSONBin stores the list of your photo links so every visitor sees the same gallery.

  • Go to jsonbin.io and sign up free
  • Once logged in, click "Create Bin"
  • In the editor, paste this starting value exactly: [] (empty array)
  • Click Save
  • Copy your Bin ID from the URL — it looks like: 64a1b2c3d4e5f6789012abcd
  • Go to Account → API Keys and copy your Secret Key (starts with $2b$...)
💡 Tip: Make sure your bin is set to Private — only your secret key can write to it, but anyone can read it (needed for the gallery to load).
⚠️ JSONBin Free Plan: Allows up to 10,000 requests/month — more than enough for a business website. The gallery list is fetched once per page visit.

3 Enter Keys on Your Website

Open your website aaryan4r.github.io/SimaaryanStones and scroll to the Gallery section.

  • Click the ⚙ Admin Upload button in the gallery toolbar
  • Enter a password of your choice (protects the upload button)
  • Paste your ImgBB API Key
  • Paste your JSONBin Bin ID
  • Paste your JSONBin Secret Key
  • Click Save & Unlock Upload
  • The Upload Photos button will appear ✅
🔒 Your keys are saved only in your browser (localStorage). They are never sent anywhere except directly to ImgBB and JSONBin. Visitors cannot see them.

4 Upload Your Granite Photos

  • Click Upload Photos — select one or multiple images
  • Each photo shows an animated spinner while uploading
  • After upload, photos appear in the grid immediately
  • Hover any photo → click 🔍 View Full to open the full-screen lightbox
  • Hover any photo → click 🗑 Delete to remove it (admin only)
  • All visitors will see the photos next time they open the site

🔍 Lightbox — Full Screen Viewer

  • Click any photo (or the 🔍 View Full button) to open it full screen
  • Use ‹ › arrows to go to the previous/next photo
  • Press ←→ arrow keys on keyboard to navigate
  • Press Escape or click the ✕ button to close
  • Click anywhere outside the photo to close

⚠️ Troubleshooting

  • Upload fails: Double-check your ImgBB API key — make sure you copied it from api.imgbb.com, not from the main site
  • Gallery not loading: Check your JSONBin Bin ID and Secret Key. The bin must exist and contain [] initially
  • Photos visible to you but not others: Make sure the JSONBin bin is set to allow public reads (default setting is fine)
  • Forgot password: Open browser console → type localStorage.clear() → re-enter all keys
